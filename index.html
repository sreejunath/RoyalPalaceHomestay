About Us

Welcome to Royal Palace Home Stay, a charming family-run retreat nestled just 300 meters from the breathtaking Varkala Cliff. Our homestay offers a peaceful and serene environment, perfect for unwinding and experiencing the local culture. We pride ourselves on providing comfortable, clean, and budget-friendly accommodation. Guests can enjoy easy access to Varkala Beach and the Cliff, both just a short, pleasant walk away. Come and experience the warm hospitality of Kerala with us!
