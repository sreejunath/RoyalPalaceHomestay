About Us

Welcome to Royal Palace Home Stay, a charming family-run retreat nestled just 300 meters from the breathtaking Varkala Cliff. Our homestay offers a peaceful and serene environment, perfect for unwinding and experiencing the local culture. We pride ourselves on providing comfortable, clean, and budget-friendly accommodation. Guests can enjoy easy access to Varkala Beach and the Cliff, both just a short, pleasant walk away. Come and experience the warm hospitality of Kerala with us!

Nearby Attractions

Varkala Beach

A beautiful stretch of golden sand, perfect for swimming and sunbathing.

Janardhana Swami Temple

A historic 2000-year-old Vishnu temple, significant for pilgrims.

Kappil Lake

Where the backwaters meet the Arabian Sea, offering serene boat rides.

Sivagiri Mutt

The final resting place of the great social reformer Sree Narayana Guru.

Anchuthengu Fort

Built by the British in the late 1600s, Anjuthengu Fort is a historic seaside fort known for its colonial past and scenic coastal views

Varkala Cliff Walk

A picturesque promenade along the cliff edge with stunning ocean views.
